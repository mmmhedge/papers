# How Cars24 scales conversations and builds faster with OpenAI

**Source:** OpenAI
**Published:** 2026-07-16 | **Link:** [https://openai.com/index/cars24](https://openai.com/index/cars24)

## Summary
Cars24 deployed OpenAI-powered voice and chat agents to automate customer conversations at scale, processing over 1 million monthly conversation minutes. The system recovered 12% of previously lost leads and enabled teams across the company to integrate agentic workflows into their operations.

## Key Points
- Cars24 built voice and chat agents using OpenAI's models to handle high-volume customer interactions
- The agents process 1M+ conversation minutes monthly, indicating significant operational scale
- The system recovered 12% of lost leads, demonstrating measurable business value
- Agentic workflows were adopted across multiple teams, suggesting broad internal deployment beyond initial use cases
- This represents a production-grade implementation of LLM agents in a customer-facing, revenue-impacting context

## Why It Matters
This case study demonstrates practical viability of AI agents in high-stakes, high-volume customer service environments. The 12% lead recovery metric shows direct ROI, making a concrete argument for enterprise adoption of agentic systems. It also illustrates how agents can be integrated across organizational workflows rather than confined to single departments, relevant to enterprise AI scaling challenges.

This relates to broader work on [[multi-agent-systems]] in production settings and [[large-language-models]] for real-time dialogue. Similar customer service automation has been explored in other verticals, but Cars24's scale and cross-team adoption extends beyond isolated chatbot deployments.

OpenAI agents recovered 12% of lost leads for Cars24, processing 1M+ monthly conversations at production scale.
