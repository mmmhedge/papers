# Securing the future of AI agents

**Source:** Google DeepMind
**Published:** 2026-06-16 | **Link:** [https://deepmind.google/blog/securing-the-future-of-ai-agents/](https://deepmind.google/blog/securing-the-future-of-ai-agents/)

## Summary
Google DeepMind outlines a systematic approach to securing AI agent systems through an "AI Control Roadmap" that integrates traditional safety mechanisms with continuous real-time monitoring. The work addresses the critical challenge of ensuring AI agents remain controllable and safe as they operate autonomously in complex environments.

## Key Points
- Introduction of an **AI Control Roadmap** as a structured framework for agent security
- Combination of **traditional safeguards** (constraints, access controls, behavioral boundaries) with **real-time monitoring systems**
- Focus on internal system security rather than just external deployment safety
- Emphasis on proactive detection and intervention capabilities
- Integration of multiple security layers to prevent misalignment or unintended behavior

## Why It Matters
As AI agents become increasingly autonomous and capable, security mechanisms must evolve beyond static constraints. Real-time monitoring paired with traditional safeguards creates a defense-in-depth approach that can catch anomalies or drift in agent behavior before harmful actions occur. This is particularly important for high-stakes domains where agent errors carry significant consequences.
